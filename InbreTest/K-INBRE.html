K-INBRE Annual Symposium

The K-INBRE symposium is a professional research meeting with the goal of providing an opportunity for Kansas scientists to present their original research in both poster and oral format. Students present side-by-side with working scientists providing opportunities for networking and future collaboration. The Symposium provides an authentic experience to students who may not have attended a major conference.

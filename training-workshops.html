About

The DELTA training workshop will be on use of the “Delta Cluster Spectrum Application Center”. Please see Flyer attached. We are limiting the number of available slots to 15 for the first training session. Account and startup allocation will be provided to all attendees.


We would like to invite researchers, faculty, graduate and undergraduate students from our PUI institutions to attend.

This DELTA training workshop will be on the use of the "Delta Cluster Spectrum Application Center" and considered an introductory class towards using DELTA and learning about access to other HPC systems available as well.


When & Where

This DELTA training workshop is being held on Friday, May 26, 2017 at LSU in Baton Rouge, Digital Media Center conducted by the LBRN Bioinformatics, Biostatistics, and Computational Biology Core..

Directions and parking

Directions to the Training - the training is being held within the LSU Digital Media Center Training room 1034. When you enter the building (follow the signs when you approach the building to the front door, there is a round reception desk, go to the right down the hall to the training room, room 1034. There are restrooms nearby. Baton Rouge Traffic is notoriously difficult in the morning across the I-10 Mississippi bridge if you're coming from the north, actually taking the old bridge is a viable alternative. Coming from New Orleans or I-12, we recommend taking the Acadian Exit to go down Stanford and then by the lakes, and then turning left in front of the LSU Emerging Tech Center and then right into the Parking Lot for CCT. Be sure NOT to park in any red marked lots, the regular spaces are fine.
